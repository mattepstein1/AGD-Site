Garage Door repairs
__



Get your garage door repaired by experts.

Garage doors are often the main entrance for a house and get a lot of use on a daily basis. It’s a good idea to have a regular service done on your garage door which can help you avoid inconvenient down-time.

However, often things happen that require repairs, such as broken garage door spring, cables coming off, wheels come out of the tracks etc. When this happens, we can come and fix the problem for you asap, but we always look for the root of the problem to ensure it doesn’t happen again and your garage door repair lasts.

If your car is trapped inside the garage, or if the garage door is stuck open and posing a security risk, we can come out and do your garage door repairs as top priority to ensure safety and cause as little disruption to you as possible.

  • Garage door spring replacements for tilt and sectional doors.
  • Cables off the rollers.
  • Wheels come out of tracks.
  • Door stops going up or down.
  • Door goes up and down on its own.
  • We find and fix annoying noises.
  • Re-tension springs.
  • And anything else you can imagine.

Garage opener repairs
__



Get your garage opener repaired by experts.

Automatic garage door openers do a lot of work and need to be kept in good condition. The best way to do this is to keep your door balanced with regular servicing otherwise they can take more strain than they were designed for and wear out faster than they should.

Repairs can be done to the more common models, but we always try and find the root of the problem first, often issues can go away once the garage door has been adjusted properly.

It is worth noting that on the older models some parts are no longer available, we will always advise of this and recommend suitable alternative options where needed.

  • Garage door spring replacements for tilt and sectional doors.
  • Cables off the rollers.
  • Wheels come out of tracks.
  • Door stops going up or down.
  • Door goes up and down on its own.
  • We find and fix annoying noises.
  • Re-tension springs.
  • And anything else you can imagine.
