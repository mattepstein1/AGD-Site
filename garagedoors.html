Sectional Doors
__



There are many reasons to want a new garage door. We offer a fully packaged service from beginning to end backed by local warranty and local reputation. We use the exact same manufacturers as the big brands and because we are not a franchise, we can recommend and use different manufacturers for different requirements, often resulting in more competitive prices. Whatever your reason for your new garage door, we can help. We install everything from colorsteel, cedar, 2.0 aluminum sectional doors and colorsteel or aluminum roller doors. All our doors are made in Auckland and all sectional doors can be insulated and have windows added for extra light.

Wood Tech

Flat Woodgrain

The most modern contemporary and minimal style of all, the flat woodgrain door is the choice for most new builds due to its unfussy and minimal design. Available in any of the colorsteel shades or powder coated to suit your taste.

Fineline

A contemporary version on the rib line door, but with a more modern feel. This would compliment a brick and tile or a rendered property perfectly. The fine lines on a fine line door make your garage look sleek and modern.

Ribline

Made to replicate the weatherboard look, rib line doors work well in a wide variety of settings. Available in a woodgrain or a smooth finish it is a very adaptable door that will never go out of style.

Pressed Panel

A classic style that still looks great today, pressed panel doors are able to show off the garage in a way no other door can. Able to look great in any use style, a very versatile style.

0.95 Flatline Smooth
